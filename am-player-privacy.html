Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

AM Player ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

AM Player does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The App works completely offline and does not require account creation or sign-in.

Permissions

The App requests access to media files stored on your device only to:

  • Play your selected media.

These files never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server.

AM Player does not sell, share, or transfer any user information to third parties.

Advertisements

The App does not display advertisements.

Analytics

The App does not use analytics services, tracking technologies, or crash reporting tools.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.
